Snake Rush

Install (unavailable now - Conceptual Phase)

Snake Rush is currently in an intensive conceptual work phase.

The project is evolving from a dynamic idea into a full-fledged turn-based game. I am focusing on creating a unique experience that combines classic themes with modern strategic mechanics. I want it to be more than just a typical board game where the player's piece is forced into a linear movement path.

Current Stage: Dice Mechanics Development

The key point of development right now is the dice roll system—the heart of board games. I am working on an algorithm that will ensure fair yet exciting gameplay, serving as the foundation for every player move.

Graphic Concept and Game World

Parallel to the code, the project's visual setting is being created:

  • Board Design: I am creating a unique tile layout that will force players to plan their moves several turns in advance.
  • Game Pieces: I am designing distinctive character models that will give the game a unique style and make it easier to identify the situation on the board.
  • Paths and Navigation: I am defining complex movement paths that will make every session unique.

Gameplay Mechanics Plan

Snake Rush will be based on a turn-based system. The main premise is strategic position management on the board. Every turn is a new decision, and a precisely planned path for the pieces is meant to be the key to ultimate success.
