Struggling to find the word that will give you victory? Is your rack full of difficult letters? Scrabble Helper was created to take your game to the next level. WHY CHOOSE SCRABBLE HELPER?

Unlike ordinary search tools, our app is powered by advanced algorithms that not only find words but also teach you how to play better. It’s a powerful tool that works completely offline, giving you access to the best dictionaries anytime, anywhere.

UNIQUE EXCHANGE STRATEGY

Stuck with only vowels or rare consonants? Our unique "Exchange Strategy" algorithm analyzes your letters and tells you exactly which ones to exchange to maximize your chances in the next turn.

KEY FEATURES:

  • Powerful Anagram Generator: Enter your letters and our engine instantly generates a list of all possible words, sorted by length and point value.
  • Smart Board Integration: Don’t limit yourself to just your letters! Enter characters already on the board and the app will show you how to anchor your tiles for record-breaking scores.
  • Professional Dictionaries: We support comprehensive and up-to-date Polish and English dictionaries. Play with confidence knowing every word is valid.
  • 100% Offline Privacy: Your data stays on your device. The app does not require internet access to search entries, making it fast, reliable, and discreet.

EXPAND YOUR VOCABULARY

Scrabble Helper is not just about winning – it’s an educational tool. Discover words you never knew existed and enrich your language with every search. It’s the perfect way to train before a tournament or simply have more fun with family and friends.

MINIMALISTIC AND FAST INTERFACE

Designed with the user in mind, the app features a clean, modern interface. No clutter, no unnecessary steps – just the words you need, exactly when you need them.

HOW TO USE – STEP BY STEP:

  1. Select your preferred language (Polish or English).
  2. Enter the letters from your rack.
  3. (Optional) Enter letters from the board you want to connect your word with.
  4. Review the results and choose the best move!
